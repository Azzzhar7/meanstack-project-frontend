Faq's

What do you stand to gain?

The ability to confidently, play, make mistakes and learn, safely and securely, within our cryptocurrency environment to teach you how to transition to real world, everyday usage.

Why are people signing up?

More than one in three people who get involved in Cryptocurrencies know or even understand what they are doing (https://www.cardify.ai/reports/crypto) and yet in the month of February 2021 there was a 16% increase in adoption.

What is the difference between this crypto education and others online?

This tool, provides all crypto education in one location., whereas others only teach aspects of crypto. Within the CryptoPlayground you can learn to invest as well as play with non- fungible tokens, discover how to create a wallet, stake, learn about the different currencies and exchanges and more, while experimenting safely and securely on our simulated platform.

What is a simulated crypto platform?

This is created when a computer simulates in 3 dimensions a real world as well as in imaginary world environment and a person can interact with it. The experience is as close to 'real life' as possible. however, the additional benefit is that it allows learners to 'reset' the scenario and try alternative strategies and approaches safely.

What repercussions are there if I make mistakes on this platform?

There are no repercussions as we learn by making mistakes and then redoing the task to get it right. You can confidently go over and over your learning journeys and be safe in the knowledge that there are no detrimental effects within this platform.

How secure is this platform?

One of the co-founders is a US military veteran and cybersecurity expert so all personal information on the platform is secure. The learning environment is safe for you to experience this complex ecosystem.

How do I transition to real world usage?

When ready you can set up your real wallet, use a blockchain/crypto debit card and more outside of the learning environment. You can practice doing these things within the platform safely and build your confidence however once you leave the platform learning environment, you take personal responsibility for your choices.
